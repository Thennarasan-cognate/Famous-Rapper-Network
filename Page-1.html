About

Music is my life! I love to create art and I’m here to inspire others! I grew up in Hartford, but moved to Phoenix, AZ shortly after my father died and started a new life. I got into rapping while I was attending Arizona State University. I started getting more into it when I was going to Phoenix College taking music classes. Nowadays I’m involved with various forms of art. My purpose is to have a positive impact on the planet. I will live a life that would make my father proud and help millions of people live better lives.
